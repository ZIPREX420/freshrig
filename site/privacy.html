Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

What FreshRig Does

FreshRig reads hardware information from your computer — including CPU model, GPU model, RAM capacity, storage devices, network adapters, motherboard model, and audio devices — using your operating system's native APIs: Windows Management Instrumentation (WMI) on Windows, /proc + lspci on Linux, and system_profiler + sysctl on macOS.

Local Processing Only

All hardware information is processed and displayed locally on your device. FreshRig does NOT transmit, collect, store, or share your hardware data with any server, third party, or the developer.

Auto-Updater

FreshRig checks for updates by sending an HTTP GET request to GitHub Releases (https://github.com/ZIPREX420/freshrig/releases/latest/download/latest.json). This request contains your current app version, target operating system, and architecture. Your IP address is visible to GitHub's servers during this request. See GitHub's Privacy Policy.

App Installation

When you install applications through FreshRig, it uses Microsoft's winget package manager. Download requests go directly to the app publishers' servers via winget's infrastructure. FreshRig does not intermediate or log these downloads.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • No analytics
  • No cookies
  • No personal information
  • No usage statistics
  • No error reporting
  • No device fingerprinting
  • No advertising IDs

Profiles

Saved profiles (.freshrig.json files) are stored locally on your device. Shared profiles contain only app selection data and optionally basic hardware info (CPU/GPU model, RAM amount) — never unique identifiers like serial numbers or MAC addresses.

Children's Privacy

FreshRig does not knowingly collect data from children under 13 (COPPA) or under 16 (GDPR).

GDPR

As FreshRig processes no personal data beyond what is technically necessary for the auto-update check, and all hardware processing is local, data subject rights (access, deletion, portability) do not apply to locally-processed data. For the auto-updater IP exposure, the legal basis is legitimate interest (keeping your software secure and up to date). You may contact us at freshrig@proton.me.

CCPA

FreshRig does not sell, share, or collect personal information as defined by the California Consumer Privacy Act.

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
